Recommendations

  1. Long Sleeve Shirts and Jackets are selling with no influence of promotions
    • Promotional strategy:
      • Offers like buy 1 get 1 free, a pack of 2 Short Sleeve Shirts for lesser price
      • Target on what a fan likes the most on Merchandise,like a logo of Seattle Reign FC on front or the logo of Sponsors
  2. Saturday Afternoon 1pm and Sunday Evenings, tickets are likely to sell more when promotions are effective
    • Promotional Strategy: - Seasonal Tickets can use certain characters or mascots for as a “free gift with purchase”, Target College students - College and Graduate students are not attending the match on Saturday Afternoons 1pm? Distance? - Additional parking facilities during weekend

Conclusion

  • Focus on promoting the Sponsored Merchandises like Pepsi’s short sleeve shirt which is popular
    • Promotions are effective with overall increase in expenditure on merchandises
    • More promotions are required for:
      • Accessories and Jackets
  • Seattle Reign should focus on target audiences like College Students and their preferred timing
